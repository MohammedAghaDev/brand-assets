# Mohammed Agha Development Logo System

## Design Philosophy

The logo follows a modern geometric monogram style.

Goals:

- Minimal
- Premium
- Professional
- Timeless
- Recognizable
- Works from 16px to 4096px

---

## Core Principles

The logo must always be:

- Vector (SVG)
- Pixel perfect
- Symmetrical
- Clean
- No unnecessary details

---

## Logo Variants

logo.svg

Primary logo.

logo-dark.svg

For dark backgrounds.

logo-light.svg

For light backgrounds.

icon.svg

Square application icon.

favicon.svg

Browser icon.

---

## Safe Area

Always keep at least 20% empty space around the logo.

Never place text too close to the mark.
